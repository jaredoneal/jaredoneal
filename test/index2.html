Selected Projects

In here you will find some choice projects that are totally nectar, bro. These projects are by no means inclusive of my skills, but just some examples that represent my interests.

NECB TalkTracks

This project was an exercise in the iceberg theory. Meant to be used as an onboarding aid, this site is designed for admissions representatives to guide prospective students or employees through potential benefits of enrolling at the online university New England College of Business.

Golf Academy Home Page

Golf Academy required a little more finesse on the backend than it needed TLC on the front end. After some minor adjustments and some refactoring I managed to reduce page size by 50% and drastically improve Google PageSpeed and GTMetrix scores.

Brightwood College AMP Landing Page

The process of converting a landing page into Google's experimental AMP paid off with some huge dividends. The accelerated load time has led to some great results on conversions. AMP definitely has drawbacks, but anything that receives preferable Google Search treatment is going to be key in the future.
